How We Score Bias

Stance vs. Bias

Stance measures what the article says about an entity (Pro/Anti). A strong stance isn't necessarily biased if supported by facts.

Bias Risk measures how it says it. We look for manipulation, emotional language, and lack of balance.

The Council of Models

  • Stance Model: Sentiment towards political entities.
  • Subjectivity: Opinion vs. Fact ratio.
  • Loaded Language: Use of inflammatory words.
  • Balance: Presence of counter-arguments.
